NERGISI KOFTEY!

INGREDIENTS

  1. 500g ground beef (preferably with some fat)
  2. 1 medium onion, grated
  3. 2 cloves garlic, minced
  4. 1 egg
  5. 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  6. 1/4 cup water
  7. 1 teaspoon salt
  8. 1 teaspoon black pepper
  9. 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  10. 1/2 teaspoon baking soda (optional)
  11. Fresh parsley, chopped (optional)

INSTRUCTIONS

Prepare the Meat Mixture

  • In a large bowl, combine the ground beef, grated onion, and minced garlic
  • Add the egg, breadcrumbs, water, salt, black pepper, cumin, and baking soda (if using). Mix thoroughly until all ingredients are well combined

    • Shape the Köfte

  • Wet your hands to prevent the mixture from sticking. Take small portions of the meat mixture and shape them into oval or oblong meatballs, about the size of a small egg. Flatten them slightly

    • Chill

  • Place the shaped köfte on a tray and cover with plastic wrap. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to help them firm up

    • Cook

  • Heat a grill, grill pan, or skillet over medium-high heat. Brush lightly with oil to prevent sticking
  • Cook the köfte for about 3-4 minutes on each side, until they are well browned and cooked through

    • Serve
  • Serve the İnegöl Köfte hot, with a side of rice or bulgur pilaf, and a fresh salad. You can also serve them with pita bread and a dollop of yogurt sauce
